Café · Bar · Events · Oceanbeach Rd

Small space.
Big presence.

*Special Mention is a café, bar and community venue tucked into Oceanbeach Rd, Arataki — Mount Maunganui. It's the kind of place you walk past and think is a convenience store, then step inside and immediately wonder why you haven't been coming here your whole life.

By day: great coffee, cinnamon buns, poached eggs, Reuben, summer bowls and a matcha coconut that people keep coming back for. By night: wine, events, live music and the kind of crowd that makes it worthwhile. We switch up the menu, keep it local, and turn the music up when we feel like it.
